Welcome to BSLog!


BSLog is a web application designed to reinforce the fundamentals of diabetes management for Type I diabetes - logging your blood sugars and counting carbohydrates.
With the assistance of BSLog's carbohydrate counting and bolus calculator, the insulin you take will be the most efficient amount to keep your blood sugars in range to meet your HgA1c goals.
